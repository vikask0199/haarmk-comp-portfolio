Company Policies

At HAARMK Pvt. Ltd., we prioritize the security and privacy of your data through a comprehensive Data Security and Privacy Policy. This includes stringent encryption standards for data in transit and at rest, robust access controls, regular security audits, and a well-defined incident response plan to address any potential data breaches. Our commitment to service excellence is articulated in our Service Level Agreement (SLA), encompassing clearly defined service levels, uptime guarantees, and response times. We maintain transparent communication with customers regarding scheduled maintenance, and our SLA includes compensation mechanisms for service disruptions beyond agreed-upon levels. In adherence to ethical business practices, our Acceptable Use Policy establishes guidelines for the responsible use of company resources, while also delineating restrictions on activities such as hacking and spamming. Consequences for policy violations are clearly outlined, ensuring a secure and ethical operating environment. Compliance with industry regulations is paramount. We adhere to industry-specific standards, conducting regular audits and implementing protocols to handle compliance-related inquiries. Our commitment extends to employee conduct, outlined in our Employee Code of Conduct, which sets expectations for professional behavior, addresses conflicts of interest, and provides guidelines for the ethical use of technology. Protecting intellectual property is central to our operations. Our Intellectual Property Policy safeguards company-owned assets and provides guidelines for the use of third-party software. We encourage employees to report potential intellectual property infringements promptly. To manage change effectively, our Change Management Policy outlines procedures for testing and implementing software changes, accompanied by communication plans to keep customers informed about updates. We prioritize customer support, with detailed procedures for handling inquiries, robust communication protocols during disruptions, and regular feedback mechanisms. Data integrity is maintained through our Data Backup and Recovery Policy, which includes regular schedules for backups, off-site storage procedures, and rigorous testing of recovery processes. Our Subscription and Billing Policies ensure transparency, with clear terms for subscription plans, billing cycles, and pricing, along with procedures for handling billing disputes and issuing refunds. These policies, regularly reviewed and updated, form the foundation of our commitment to delivering secure, reliable, and innovative cloud solutions. We engage legal professionals to ensure compliance with all relevant laws and regulations, providing you with confidence in our services.
