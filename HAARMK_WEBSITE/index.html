About Us

Established in 2020, HAARMK Infotech Pvt Ltd is a dynamic and forward-thinking digital solutions provider based in India. With a firm commitment to enhancing and promoting Digital India, we have evolved into a leading digital content creator with a diverse portfolio of 15+ products and services.
Our Vision & Specializations

At HAARMK, we envision a digitally empowered future, where innovative solutions drive efficiency and connectivity across industries. Our mission is to contribute to the growth of Digital India by providing cutting-edge Software as a Service (SAAS) solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

We specialize in offering SAAS solutions for various sectors, including hospital management, school management, HRMS, WorkSuite, and transport management. Our solutions are designed to streamline operations, foster collaboration, and drive digital transformation in organizations of all sizes.
